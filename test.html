BSSC is an only organization which exists in BINUS SQUARE. BSSC was established in 2010, the same year in which BINUS Square was opened. The main purpose of BSSC is to hold creative and innovative events that are able to develop the soft skills and hard skills of boarders who live in BINUS Square. Our big events troughout the year are BSSC Festival, BSSC Earth Hour, BSSC Squarelympic, BSSC EXPO, and BSSC Wonder.
